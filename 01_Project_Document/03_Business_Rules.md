# BUSINESS RULES

---

## Authentication Rules

BR-001

Every employee must login using Employee ID.

---

BR-002

Only HR and Admin can reset employee passwords.

---

BR-003

Employee cannot edit company profile information.

---

BR-004

Only Admin can edit employee profile.

---

BR-005

After 5 wrong password attempts account will be locked for 30 minutes.

---

BR-006

Inactive employees cannot login.

---

## Employee Rules

BR-007

Every employee will have one unique Employee ID.

---

BR-008

Employee ID can never be reused.

---

BR-009

Every employee must belong to one department.

---

BR-010

Every employee must have one reporting manager.

---

## Lead Rules

BR-011

Every lead must have one unique Lead ID.

---

BR-012

One lead can only be assigned to one employee at a time.

---

BR-013

Only Admin and Team Leader can transfer leads.

---

BR-014

Leads can never be permanently deleted.

---

BR-015

Employee cannot delete lead history.

---

BR-016

Every lead activity must be recorded.

---

BR-017

Every employee receives 300 leads per day.

---

BR-018

Every employee must complete minimum 200 calls daily.

---

BR-019

If customer doesn't answer, maximum 2 retry calls.

---

BR-020

No Response leads move to Recycle after business rule approval.

---
